    Welcome to the Home of The Hanover Hawks

    What are the Hanover Boys Hockey Boosters?

    The Hanover Boys Hockey Boosters is a dedicated group of parents, alumni, and community members who support the Hanover High School boys hockey team. Our mission is to enhance the hockey experience for players and fans alike through fundraising, event planning, and community engagement. The boys hockey team has performed many fundraisers over the years to support their journey to the championship. Some events the boys have organized include the yearly street hockey tournament, Bruins ticket raffles, and Helping to shovel around town during the winter months.

    How Can You Help?

    There are many ways to support the Hanover Boys Hockey Boosters! Whether you're a parent, alumni, or community member, your involvement makes a difference. Here are some ways you can help:

    • Volunteer your time for events and fundraisers
    • Attend games and show your support for the team
    • Make a donation to the boosters to help fund team activities
